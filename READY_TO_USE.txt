═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PATIENT BOOKING (No Login Needed):
  
  1. Land on homepage → See beautiful hospital showcase
  2. Click "Book Appointment" button
  3. Step 1: Select department (Laboratory/Imaging/Consultation)
  4. Step 2: Pick date and time (available slots shown)
  5. Step 3: Enter name, phone, optional problem description
  6. Confirm booking
  7. See booking ID and "Print Receipt" button
  8. Done! No logout needed.

ADMIN DASHBOARD (Manage Appointments):

  1. Land on homepage
  2. Click "Staff" button (top right)
  3. Select "Admin" role
  4. Click "Login"
  5. See live appointment queue
  6. View patient details, problem description, home address
  7. Click "Check In" to mark as completed
  8. Click "Cancel" to cancel appointment
  9. View analytics and completed history
  10. Click "Logout" when done

DOCTOR DASHBOARD (Review Patients):

  1. Land on homepage

🎯 CUSTOMIZATION:
